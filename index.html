03 | Pine Setu pre-launch framework

Design the 100K journey before launch.

Pine Setu is not live yet, so this is not a performance claim. It is a pre-launch measurement and activation framework for the September ambition. Database scale, verified registration and active use should be tracked separately.

September pre-launch ambition
100K

alumni in first 3 months

Before launch, Pine should define whether 100K means records sourced, alumni contacted, verified registrations, completed profiles or active users. The strategy should report each layer separately.

01

Identify

Build alumni cohorts from university networks, founder relationships, associations, referrals, events and compliant data partnerships.

02

Verify

Confirm identity, institution, year, career stage, location and consent so the database becomes trusted intelligence, not a raw list.

03

Activate

Use chapters, opportunities, peer discovery, recognition, events and career outcomes to create reasons for alumni to return.

04

Monetise

Offer subscription access to verified alumni intelligence, engagement workflows and curated matching. Avoid positioning it as raw data sales.

04 | 90-day execution

Build, activate, then convert.

The first quarter should create a working revenue system, not a large volume of disconnected marketing activity. The plan is designed around the 45 to 60 day sales cycle without pretending marketing controls every closure.

Days 1 to 30

Foundation

  • Audit website, analytics, CRM, social presence, content and sales handoff.
  • Finalise ICPs, account tiers and first geography sequence.
  • Build service-specific messaging, proof assets and case-study structure.
  • Map Tier 1 and Tier 2 accounts with buyer contacts and buying triggers.
  • Define Pine Setu onboarding layers and pre-launch cohorts.
Milestone: Tracking live, account list ready, messaging approved.
Days 31 to 60

Market activation

  • Launch founder-led LinkedIn, email and warm-intro outreach.
  • Publish landing pages, founder POVs and proof-led content.
  • Run one focused webinar or university roundtable.
  • Start retargeting, nurture sequences and sales enablement follow-ups.
  • Launch Pine Setu pilot cohorts with clear Identify, Verify and Activate metrics.
Milestone: 3 to 5 qualified B2B conversations in motion.
Days 61 to 90

Optimise and convert

  • Double down on best-performing account segments and messages.
  • Move qualified conversations into proposals, pilots or MOU discussions.
  • Document objections, proof gaps and sales-cycle blockers.
  • Publish first formal Pine Careers proof asset or case narrative.
  • Create Q2 scale plan for geography, budget, team and Pine Setu growth.
Milestone: 1 to 2 active proposals. Signed client is a stretch outcome.
✓

Unfair advantage: The founder network is not separate from marketing. It should be the highest-trust acquisition channel, supported by content, account intelligence, landing pages and structured follow-up.
